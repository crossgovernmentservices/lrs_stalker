Description

This hour-long course will will help you to understand unconscious bias and how it affects attitudes, behaviours and decision-making.

You will learn practical techniques that will help you reduce the impact of unconscious bias and so, ultimately, create a more inclusive and diverse workforce.

This course is particularly useful if you are a line manager.

  • which techniques to use to help you identify, challenge, prevent and remove areas of unconscious bias in the workplace
